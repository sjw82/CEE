The Center for Empathy Education (CEE) is a consulting firm which provides resources, workshops, and counseling to assist parents, educators, medical professionals, and employers in implementing and teaching empathy across multiple vectors, from daily interactions to policy decisions. Our services vary greatly based on individual needs, but our core focus is working towards equity and compassion through empathy in every setting. Our aim is to affirm a culture of inclusion and to inspire productive dialogue by bringing empathy into the lives of as many individuals as possible with the science of social emotional learning.
